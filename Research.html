Research


I’m passionate about improving suicide risk assessment and intervention. I research individual characteristics that may affect one’s vulnerability or resilience in the face of negative life events, stressors, or other triggers. In particular, I am interested in understanding how perfectionism may affect responses to certain negative life events, and how this might contribute to poorer mental health outcomes —– up to and including suicide risk. Relatedly, I evaluate how we can best train mental health professionals to help clients at risk of suicide cope with negative life events.
